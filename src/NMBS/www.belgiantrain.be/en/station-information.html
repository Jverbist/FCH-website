Adjusted opening hours ticket offices 10/5 and 16/8
On the holidays Friday 10 May and Friday 16 August, we will adjust the opening hours of most ticket offices:
  • Antwerp-Central, Bruges, Brussels-Central, Brussels-Schuman, Brussels-Midi (national ticket offices) and Liege-Guillemins will keep their opening hours of every other Friday.
  • All other ticket offices operate according to their Saturday opening hours: is your counter normally open or closed on Saturday? Then it will be the same on these two Fridays.
